Institutional Design

Eureka International School

Crafting Space
With Purpose

An architecture student at BIT Mesra, Ranchi, I view every site as a living narrative waiting to be told. My work is a conscious dialogue between the structured precision of institutional design and the evocative, organic patterns of my cultural heritage. I don't just build enclosures; I choreograph the interplay of light, wind, and human movement to create spaces that resonate with purpose.

Design Manifesto

Climate as the Primary Architect

Leveraging passive cooling and solar orientation not as "add-ons," but as the foundational logic of the built form.

Institutional Fluidity

Reimagining rigid educational blocks as adaptive learning ecosystems that encourage spontaneous interaction.

Mathematical Heritage

Integrating the fractal symmetry of traditional folk art into contemporary structural geometry.
